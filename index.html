About

HP

This game has two types of damage that occur when hit: White and Red.
  • White Damage can be gradually recovered over time when maintaining a state of not being hit/guarding.
  • Red Damage can not be recovered naturally. Reduce the opponent's HP to 0 using Red Damage to win the round.

MP

Using MP moves consume a set amount of MP for each move. MP can be recovered in the following three ways:
  • Recovers naturally over time when not consumed.
  • Landing an attack that does not use MP to activate.
  • Using Conversion.

Conversion

While White Damage is active, you can use Conversion to exchange remaining White Damage and take a small amount of Red Damage in order to recover MP.
  • The amount of MP recovered depends on how much White Damage was used.
  • Conversion can be used to cancel the animation of many different actions.

Basic Actions

  • Dash: Rapidly → →
  • Backstep: Rapidly ← ←
  • Grab: A + B / ← + A + B
  • Tech Grab: A + B when grabbed
  • Conversion: B + Skills
  • Dodge: Press → while guarding
  • Guard Cancel: → + B + Skills while guarding
  • Ground/Air Tech Roll: Activated automatically after being hit while airborne.
